NATURE. REDEFINED.

Harvey Dawes grew up in a small town in the alps, surrounded by plants, trees and mountains. After getting widespread recognition for his work on depicting his surroundings, Dawes moved to London, where his mother grew up, to pursue his career as an artist. His newest collection of photographs shows what global warming has done to the world by documenting the state of many different plants.

Between 2020-11-20 and 2021-02-30, Harvey Dawes plant-based art will be dipslayed at Intelligent REDEFINED., in the exhibition NATURE. REDEFINED. All you need is a standard ticket.
